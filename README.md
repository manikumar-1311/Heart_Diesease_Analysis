❤️ Heart Disease — Simple & Impressive Overview
4
💡 What is Heart Disease?

Heart disease happens when the heart or blood vessels don’t work properly — most commonly because arteries get narrowed or blocked by fat deposits (plaque). This reduces blood flow and can lead to a heart attack.

🚩 Main Causes

High blood pressure

High cholesterol

Smoking

Diabetes

Obesity

Lack of exercise

Stress

👉 Most of these are preventable.

⚠️ Common Symptoms

Chest pain (pressure or tightness)

Shortness of breath

Fatigue

Fast or irregular heartbeat

Pain in arm, jaw, or back

🚨 Severe chest pain + sweating + nausea = Emergency

📊 Simple Risk Idea

Doctors often check:

Age

Blood pressure

Cholesterol level

Blood sugar

Heart rate

ECG results

Higher values in these increase heart disease risk.

🛡️ How to Prevent It

✅ Exercise 30 minutes daily
✅ Eat healthy (less salt & oil)
✅ Stop smoking
✅ Control BP & sugar
✅ Regular check-ups

🌟 Impressive Fact

👉 Up to 80% of heart disease cases can be prevented with healthy lifestyle changes.
